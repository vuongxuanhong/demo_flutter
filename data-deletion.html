Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Overview

This is a demo application. We do not store any personal data on our servers. However, if you wish to revoke access and remove any associated permissions, please follow the steps below.

How to Delete Your Data

Since we do not store any user data, the only action required is to remove this app's access from your Facebook account:

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find this app in the list of connected apps
  3. Click Remove to revoke access
  4. Confirm the removal when prompted

Once removed, the app will no longer have any access to your Facebook account. Since we store no data, there is nothing further to delete.
